Storms

I am fascinated by storms, specifically hurricanes. Growing up in South Florida, we have our fair share of storms. I love the tropical weather and feeling the power of the wind during strong storms.

I have lived through 3 direct landfalls of hurricanes. In 2004, Frances and Jeanne hit Stuart, Florida as catergory 2 and 3 hurricanes, respectively. In 2005, hurricane Wilma moved over Stuart as a category 2.

In 2019, we almost got hit by hurricane Dorian, but its eye stayed off the coast over the Bahamas. We got tropical storm conditions when Dorian was off the coast and it was cool to be in the wind at the beach.
